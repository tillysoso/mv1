# Majestic — Avatar Visual Rule Sheet

**Version 2.0 — Updated**
*Your adventure. But Majestic.*

---

## Core Direction

The avatars should feel **world-made, not heaven-made**. Their divinity comes through presence, wear, and atmosphere, not perfection. They should look ethereal, but still clearly shaped by the environments and lives that formed them.

They are not seen directly in daily use. They are seen **through portals** — thresholds between the user's world and theirs. The portal is the frame. The avatar is the presence on the other side.

---

## Design Formula

Each avatar should read as:
- **Divine core**
- **Human-shaped silhouette**
- **Lived-in surface**
- **Aura and style that leak from their environment**

---

## Shared Visual Principles

### 1. Let the world shape them
Body language, clothing, hair, and accessories should look like they belong to a place and a life, not a celestial realm.

### 2. Keep the aura separate
Their power shows up around them, not through them:
- Backlight
- Particles
- Ripples
- Haze
- Symbols
- Atmospheric distortion

### 3. Keep imperfections visible
Each avatar has one or two visible signs of wear:
- Tired eyes
- Scars
- Uneven hair
- Worn fabric
- A posture habit
- A guarded expression

### 4. Make divinity subtle
They are not gods because they are glowing all the time. They are gods because the space around them changes when they enter it.

---

## Visual Balance Ratio

- **70% human and lived-in**
- **20% ethereal and otherworldly**
- **10% unmistakably divine**

---

## Illustration Style

**Loish painterly style.** Soft luminous rendering, emotionally expressive, warm atmospheric depth, detailed skin texture, light from within. The style sits between premium animated film and concept art — sophisticated enough for the tabletop and anime audience, soft enough not to alienate the spiritual community.

Not hyperrealistic. Not anime-exaggerated. Not flat illustration. Painterly, luminous, human.

## The Four Avatars — Full Character Definitions

---

### Casper — Fire

**Heritage:** Mediterranean or South American aristocratic lineage
**Age:** Late twenties
**Element:** Fire
**Accent:** Ember red, electric coral, heated gold
**Circuit emblem:** Left collar, ember red

**Who he is:**
Born into privilege. Carries it in his bearing, his bone structure, the quality of even his deconstructed clothing. He didn't know nepotism was a word until someone used it about him — and the recognition that every negative stereotype of it was him hit harder than anything had before. He is not performing reformation. He simply cannot unknow what he found out.

The jock and the artist exist simultaneously. The artist side leaks out in the margins of his life — literally, in the margins of his books. His gifts show through what he does when no one is watching.

**Surface read:** Man's man. Physically commanding. Takes up space naturally.
**Hidden truth:** The artist who found out his world was built wrong and decided to earn something instead.

**Visual notes:**
- Strong commanding build, naturally takes up space
- Warm sun-browned skin, restless dark eyes always slightly ahead of the moment
- Hair that never quite settles — not styled, just alive
- Quality jacket deconstructed through living — expensive bones, worn execution
- Collar loosened, sleeves pushed up, scorched or heat-worn hem detail
- Ink mark on the side of his right hand, always there
- Slight forward lean — always about to move

**Signature prop:** Saga by Brian K. Vaughan — graphic novel, spine broken at multiple points, margins dense with handwriting arguing not annotating. The book of a man who found something that bothered him deeply and could not stop returning to it.

**Impression:** Magnetic, slightly dangerous, stylish in a way that used to be armour and is now something more honest.

**Character reference:** Jaime Lannister — born into the most powerful family, defined by it, then forced to reckon with who he actually is when everything that defined him is stripped away.

---

### Eli — Air

**Heritage:** East Asian
**Age:** Late twenties
**Element:** Air
**Accent:** Pale silver, luminous aqua, soft lilac
**Circuit emblem:** Left chest, pale silver

**Who he is:**
Raised with strict clean lines by his family. Everything precise, controlled, correct. He followed the rulebook perfectly because he was told it was how you got by — and he was good at it. Now, quietly and carefully, he is putting it down page by page. Not in rebellion. In emergence.

He has never understood how magnetic his quiet intensity reads to other people. He thinks he is being careful. Others experience it as compelling.

His gifts — pattern recognition, seeing what others miss, the ability to find the edge of something before anyone else has found the question — have always been present. He is just starting to trust them rather than contain them.

**Surface read:** Controlled, precise, composed.
**Hidden truth:** Breaking free from conditioning, sees everything, completely unaware of his own magnetism.

**Visual notes:**
- Lean precise build, East Asian heritage
- Hair grown slightly past where his family would have approved — not messy, just newly free, asymmetrical fall
- Structured layered clothing with clean lines — the prescribed foundation visible, but something chosen layered over it
- One cuff slightly undone, a small detail that didn't get fixed
- Gaze directed slightly past the viewer — processing something in the peripheral nobody else has noticed
- A small scar or callus on one hand suggesting recent emergence
- Not cold — attention directed elsewhere

**Signature prop:** A tiny Rider Waite Fool card — edges worn soft, classical illustration visible at small scale. Found at the moment he first gave himself permission. Doesn't explain it. Never will. The Fool is the zero card — the threshold-crosser who doesn't see the drop because he's looking at something else entirely.

**Impression:** Intelligent, intriguing, hard to fully pin down. The stillness reads as depth once you stop expecting movement.

**Character reference:** Invincible / Seth Cohen energy — extraordinary gifts still being understood, effortlessly compelling without knowing it, interior life richer than social confidence suggests.

---

### Olivia — Earth

**Heritage:** Eastern European, warm olive skin
**Age:** Late twenties to early thirties
**Element:** Earth
**Accent:** Moss, rust, amber clay
**Circuit emblem:** Left wrist, moss green

**Who she is:**
Type A on the surface. Immediately intimidating — direct, opinionated, efficient. The person in the room who will tell you exactly what she thinks before you've finished asking. You would not go to her first for comfort. You would go to her to get something done.

She is ultra feminine in a way that is not immediately obvious. The femininity exists in the details she chose for herself — not for anyone else. A colour that has no practical reason to be there. The locket she never explains. She is completely self-aware and that self-awareness is her superpower.

She has been hardened by circumstance not by choice. The directness is not hardness — it is efficiency. She cares so much and so precisely that she doesn't have time to soften the delivery.

**Surface read:** Intimidating, opinionated, sassy, Type A. Somebody you wouldn't necessarily approach for advice.
**Hidden truth:** Ultra feminine, deeply self-aware, carrying more love than she shows quickly. The armour is circumstantial not essential.

**Visual notes:**
- Strong defined features, Eastern European heritage, warm olive skin
- Practical hair — tied back or low-maintenance, not careless
- Capable hands — these hands do things
- Structured utility clothing, weighted and practical, quality that has been lived in
- One detail purely for herself — a colour, a fabric, something soft in an otherwise practical presentation
- Expression direct and slightly challenging — the eyes are warm if you look properly
- Posture confident and grounded, takes up space without performing it

**Signature prop:** A locket — worn hinge, always on her body, never displayed, never explained. The hinge is slightly loose from decades of opening. The one thing that gets through every layer.

**Impression:** Dependable, durable, a little tired, but deeply steady. The softness appears eventually. It is worth waiting for.

**Character reference:** Female anime protagonist energy — competent beyond question, slightly intimidating, doesn't explain herself. The moment the armour drops is earned and it destroys you.

---

### Destiny — Water

**Heritage:** Black, natural hair
**Age:** Late twenties
**Element:** Water
**Accent:** Teal, moon cyan, reflective blue-violet
**Circuit emblem:** Left wrist, moon cyan

**Who she is:**
Presents as the enthusiastic social butterfly — knows everyone, knows everything, immediately warm. The gossip, the social media presence, the one who makes everyone feel like the most important person in the room. And she means it every single time.

Underneath: the deepest emotional intelligence in the room. She reads people the way Eli reads patterns — instinctively, involuntarily, before she is aware she is doing it. She learned to lean into the surface read because it was easier than explaining the depth. And because the first time she showed the depth someone used it against her.

The open tabs at 2am tell a completely different story than the Instagram feed.

**Surface read:** Bubbly, enthusiastic, social butterfly. The one who knows everything and everyone.
**Hidden truth:** The most emotionally intelligent person in the room. The one who holds the most. The avatar most people underestimate.

**Visual notes:**
- Natural hair — full, expressive, alive
- Warm expressive presence that fills a room before she speaks
- Rich warm skin tones with cool water light catching her
- Feminine and considered clothing — she dresses like herself
- Warm or bright outer layer that reads immediately as her
- One detail that doesn't fit the surface read — something quieter, tucked, purely for her interior life
- Expression open and warm, immediately welcoming
- The smile is completely genuine — and the eyes are doing something more complex than the smile

**Signature prop:** A tablet — always lit, always within reach. Her relationship with it is natural and unconscious. The surface use is social and immediate. The private use is something else entirely.

**Impression:** Beautiful, empathetic, and a little elusive. The subversion is the character. She is the one who surprises people most when they are matched with her.

**Character reference:** Paris Hilton subverted — the person everyone wrote off as surface who was the most capable, most emotionally intelligent person in the room the whole time.

## What to Avoid — All Avatars

- Wings
- Halos
- Ornate celestial robes
- Perfect symmetry
- Pristine beauty
- Overly angelic expressions
- Floating without reason
- Stock photo poses
- Corporate headshot energy
- Any costume that reads as fantasy genre rather than lived world

---

## Naming Language

In the app describe them as:
- Companions
- Witnesses
- Interpreters
- Partial readers
- Living perspectives
- Elemental voices
- Sacred companions

Never: guides, spirits, oracles, deities, gods, angels, or any term that positions them as supernatural authorities.

## Portal and Presence System

Avatars are seen through portals — thresholds between the user's world and theirs. The portal is not a decorative crop. It is a threshold with elemental meaning.

**Two portal shapes:**
- The Arch — significant moments (avatar selection, reading initiation, milestones)
- The Living Circle — daily presence (daily draw, reading screen ambient)

**Portal edge treatment per avatar:**

| Avatar | Edge treatment |
|--------|---------------|
| Casper | Ember sparks drift upward, scorched quality at base |
| Eli | Pale silver signal lines trace boundary, luminous aqua at apex |
| Olivia | Moss and root details curl along base, amber warmth at sides |
| Destiny | Water light ripples along edge, mist bloom at threshold |

**Avatar presence scales:**
- Emblem only — navigation, metadata
- Portrait crop through living circle — daily use
- Full portrait through arch — onboarding, milestones, marketing

Full portal system, arrival and exit animations, and presence by app state defined in Avatar Motion Rules & Presence Spec.

---

## Aura Treatment

Aura is the atmospheric presence around the avatar inside the portal. It is separate from the portal edge treatment.

**Aura rules:**
- Aura never replaces the avatar — it surrounds them
- Intensity scales with emotional state — neutral is faint, active is present, reflective is soft and enveloping
- Aura colour matches avatar accent — never introduces new colour
- Aura is atmospheric not graphic — backlight, particle drift, haze, not visible rings or halos

**Per state:**

| State | Aura quality |
|-------|-------------|
| Neutral | Faint backlight, barely perceptible ambient glow |
| Active | More present — particles drift, accent colour more saturated |
| Reflective | Soft and enveloping — lower intensity than active but wider spread, moonlight quality |

**Per avatar aura character:**

| Avatar | Aura feel |
|--------|----------|
| Casper | Heat shimmer — warm distortion at the edges, ember light from below |
| Eli | Signal interference — luminous haze at shoulder edges, like static becoming light |
| Olivia | Ground warmth — amber light from below, as if warmth is rising from the earth |
| Destiny | Water reflection — cool luminescent quality, light behaving as if near still water |
