## Overview

The Majestic colour system is built around one constant and four variables.

**The constant:** Majestic purple — `#9500FF`. Always present. The brand underneath everything.

**The variables:** Four elemental accent sets — one per avatar. Applied as a gradient wash over the purple base. The purple grounds every surface in Majestic. The elemental wash places it in a specific world.

The result: every card, every portal, every surface feels simultaneously like it belongs to Majestic and to an element.

---

## The Gradient Wash System

### How it works

The Majestic purple sits as the base layer. The elemental accent washes across it as a semi-transparent photographic filter — pulling from the purple toward the element's colour temperature.

On cards the wash sits at the edges and fades toward the centre — the card art is always the hero, the wash frames and atmospherically colours it without obscuring the focal point.

On portal boundaries the gradient quality lives in the arch and arc edges — purple base with elemental accent washing across the boundary.

### The wash rule

- **Centre of card/surface:** Art is clearest here — wash at 0–15% opacity
- **Mid frame:** Wash builds — 20–40% opacity
- **Edges and corners:** Wash at full atmospheric presence — 50–70% opacity
- **Never:** Full opacity overlay — the art always reads through

## Gradient Overlay — Card Implementation

### The wash stack

Applied in this order on every card:

1. **Card art** — base layer, full opacity
2. **Majestic purple base wash** — `#9500FF` at 8% opacity, full card — the brand is always present
3. **Elemental gradient wash** — avatar accent gradient, edge-weighted as defined above
4. **World atmosphere layer** — subtle noise/grain texture at 5% opacity — keeps the art from feeling flat or overly filtered
5. **Card frame** — sits above all wash layers

### Opacity calibration per card type

| Card type | Wash intensity | Notes |
|-----------|---------------|-------|
| Major Arcana | 45–55% at edges | These cards carry more symbolic weight — the wash is more present |
| Minor Arcana — numbered | 35–45% at edges | Environmental and systemic — slightly lighter wash |
| Court cards | 40–50% at edges | Character-led — wash frames the figure |
| Card back | 60–70% — more present | No art to protect — the wash and the world motif are the design |

## Gradient Background — App Surfaces

The Threshold City background is itself a gradient — never a flat colour.

### Default background gradient

**Angle:** 165 degrees — top-left to bottom-right

| Stop | Position | Hex |
|------|----------|-----|
| Top | 0% | `#1A1A2E` |
| Upper mid | 30% | `#16213E` |
| Lower mid | 70% | `#0F3460` |
| Bottom | 100% | `#1A1435` |

The purple emerges at the base — always — because Majestic is always beneath the world.

### With elemental accent overlay

When an avatar is active a second gradient layer sits over the base at 15–20% opacity — pulling the background atmosphere gently toward the avatar's elemental register. The background never fully becomes the element. It leans toward it.
