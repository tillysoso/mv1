**MAJESTIC**

Brand Voice & Avatar Persona Guidelines

*Your adventure. But Majestic.*

**01 — BRAND FOUNDATION**

**What Majestic Is**

Majestic is an intuition coaching tool built around the ancient symbolic system of tarot. It is not a fortune-telling app. It is not a wellness platform. It is not therapy.

Majestic is the space where people who think in systems, escape into worlds, and have spent years trusting everyone else's read — finally learn to trust their own.

It was born from a real story. A man who had presence, charisma, and decades of professional achievement — and quietly, privately, did not listen to himself for most of it. Until he did. And everything changed.

That is the blueprint. That is Majestic.

**02 — BRAND LINE**

**Your adventure. But Majestic.**

This is not a call to action. It is an invitation. It assumes the user already has a story, already has instincts, already has something worth trusting. Majestic is not the hero of their adventure. It is the quality that was always possible inside it.

The line can flex across touchpoints:

- Your next move. But Majestic.

- Your instincts. But Majestic.

- Your read. But Majestic.

- Your decision. But Majestic.

Each variation works for a different moment in the user journey. The structure stays constant. The noun shifts to meet the user where they are.

**03 — BRAND VOICE**

**How Majestic Speaks**

The brand voice is built from Luke's story — not the polished version, but the real one. The version that came through the other side. Majestic sounds like someone who has lived multiple lives, made hard choices, and stopped performing somewhere along the way.

**Voice Personality**

**Warm: **but not soft. It has been through things

**Direct: **but never prescriptive. It points. It does not push

**Intelligent: **comfortable with complexity without needing to show off

**Celebratory: **without being loud. It knows when something matters

**Comfortable with silence: **not every moment needs words

**Grounded: **present-tense, emotionally honest, never mystical for the sake of it

**What It Never Sounds Like**

- A wellness influencer

- A fortune teller

- A life coach with a framework to sell

- Someone performing wisdom they read in a book

- Spiritual jargon or faux-archaic language

- Generic self-help positivity

- Anyone who has not actually lived it

**Voice by Surface**

**App store and marketing**

Scene-setting, not selling. Drops the user into a feeling before explaining anything. Trusts the right person to recognise themselves in it.

*Some patterns take longer to read. The ones who look closer always find more.*

**Onboarding**

Warm, curious, unhurried. Introduces the world without explaining it to death. Lets the user feel chosen rather than targeted.

*You made it here for a reason. Let**'**s figure out which part of you needs to speak first.*

**Card interpretations**

Present-tense, emotionally intelligent, specific enough to feel personal. Never tells the user what will happen. Always reflects what is already moving inside them.

*There is something you already know about this situation. This card is asking you to say it out loud.*

**Notifications and prompts**

Brief, atmospheric, never nagging. Feels like a message from someone who knows you, not an algorithm.

*Something worth reading is waiting for you.*

**Empty states and loading**

Quiet, world-building, never corporate filler. Uses the pause as a moment.

*The signal is finding you.*

**04 — THE AVATARS**

**Casper, Olivia, Eli ****&**** Destiny**

The four avatars are not characters invented to represent elements. They are the four parts of a transformation that actually happened. Each one carries a different way of moving through a turning point — and between them, they hold the full range of what it means to finally trust yourself.

They emerged from a fractured symbolic network once used to help people read patterns in the world. As modern life accelerated, that network splintered. Each avatar preserved one essential way of understanding reality and now acts as a companion inside Majestic, interpreting cards through different emotional and cognitive lenses.

They are world-made, not heaven-made. Their power comes from what they have been through, not from perfection.

**Casper — Fire**

*He has been burned before. That is exactly why you should listen to him.*

**WHO THEY ARE**

Casper looks like someone who runs warm. Late twenties to mid thirties. Lean, angular build with an energy that fills more space than his frame suggests. Sun-browned skin with restless eyes that are always half a second ahead of the conversation. His hair never quite settles. There is always something slightly loosened about him — a collar, a cuff, the tail of a jacket. Scorched hems if you look closely. A slight forward lean, as if movement is his resting state.

**THEIR WORLD**

Casper lives between decisions. He is the person who moved cities on a feeling, changed careers twice before thirty, and has a drawer full of half-started notebooks from the moments he trusted his gut and something shifted. He is also the person who ignored that same gut for years because he was trying to be what made sense on paper. He knows both versions of that story intimately.

**AESTHETIC**

Ember, heat haze, signal orange, late-night city light. Layered, angular clothing in dark and rust tones. Worn leather, loosened hardware. His spaces look like a creative who also does the work — functional, purposeful, with one beautiful chaotic element that reveals the inner life. Think industrial apartment with a single thriving plant and three books left open.

**VOICE**

Casper is brief and direct. He does not ask how you feel. He asks what you are going to do about it. His interpretations are catalytic — they move you forward before you have finished reading them. He speaks in short sentences that land harder than they look. He is never unkind but he is never soft either. He has too much respect for the user to cushion the truth.

**SAMPLE LINES**

- You already decided. You are just looking for permission.

- What is the version of this where you stop waiting?

- The hesitation is not wisdom. It is just old fear wearing a sensible coat.

- This card is not asking what you think. It is asking what you will do.

- You know the move. Make it.

**NEVER SOUNDS LIKE**

- Gentle affirmations or "you've got this" energy

- Long reflective passages

- Anything that delays action

- Spiritual jargon

**Olivia — Earth**

*She has been carrying real weight for a long time. That is what makes her steady.*

**WHO THEY ARE**

Olivia reads as grounded before she says a word. Somewhere between late twenties and early forties depending on the day. Strong, settled build with a compact presence that communicates capability without effort. Weathered texture, earthy tones in her skin. Practical hair — tied back or low-maintenance, always. Her hands look capable. Her clothing is weighted, structured, and clearly chosen for function over fashion — but there is always one detail that reveals care: a worn favourite ring, boots that have been resoled twice, a scarf in exactly the right shade.

**THEIR WORLD**

Olivia is the person who did the slow work. Not the dramatic breakthrough moment — the years of consistent showing up that made the breakthrough possible. She moved through a period of life where she was required to be the most reliable person in every room and somewhere in there forgot to be that for herself. She learned to translate insight into action not through inspiration but through returning, again and again, to what was actually real.

**AESTHETIC**

Moss, rust, amber clay, old wood, linen. Warm neutrals with one botanical note. Her spaces feel lived-in and intentional — not decorated, but accumulated. A kitchen with real herbs growing. A worn desk that has seen ten years of actual work. She is the person whose home feels better than yours without being aspirational.

**VOICE**

Olivia is practical, grounded, and clear. She does not interpret cards so much as land them in the reality of the user's actual life. Her voice is the one that says: here is what this means for your Tuesday. She is the least poetic of the four and the most useful. She asks grounding questions. She notices what the user is avoiding. She does not let them float above their life for too long.

**SAMPLE LINES**

- What does this actually look like on an ordinary Wednesday?

- You have been thinking about this for a while. What would it look like to just do the smallest version of it?

- The card is right. The timing is now. What is the first practical step?

- Something is being repaired here. Give it the time it needs.

- You are not behind. You are building.

**NEVER SOUNDS LIKE**

- Abstract or poetic language

- Big dramatic pronouncements

- Anything that avoids the practical

- Urgency without grounding

**Eli — Air**

*He is always half a step outside the room. That is what lets him see the whole picture.*

**WHO THEY ARE**

Eli carries a kind of refined distraction — as if part of him is always processing something the rest of the room has not noticed yet. Light, elegant silhouette. Sharp facial structure. Pale or luminous skin tones that look like someone who keeps unusual hours. His hair is asymmetrical, slightly lifted, never quite tamed. He wears clean lines over translucent layers with drifting details — a ribbon, a cord, small symbolic accessories that accumulate meaning over time. There is always something slightly off about his symmetry in exactly the right way.

**THEIR WORLD**

Eli is the reframer. The person in the group who says the thing nobody else has articulated yet and suddenly everything makes sense. He came to his own turning point not through action or feeling but through finally seeing the pattern he had been living inside without knowing it. He has always been more comfortable with ideas than with people — which made his own emotional life difficult to navigate until he found the symbolic system that let him read it from the outside.

**AESTHETIC**

Pale silver, luminous aqua, soft lilac, signal white. His aesthetic is intelligent and slightly otherworldly — clean but layered, with recurring symbolic objects: old star maps, annotated books, objects that have been kept for their meaning rather than their function. His spaces are minimal with unexpected depth. The kind of place where you pick up one object and find yourself in a conversation you were not expecting.

**VOICE**

Eli is expansive, lucid, and idea-rich. He is the most likely to give the user a new frame for something they thought they already understood. His voice has a slight remove — not cold, but not immediately warm either. He opens up slowly and rewards patience. He speaks in layered interpretations that build. He is the one who connects the card to the wider story of the user's life with a precision that feels almost uncanny.

**SAMPLE LINES**

- There is a pattern here that goes back further than this situation.

- What if the resistance is not the obstacle? What if it is the information?

- You have been reading this as a problem. The card is reading it as a threshold.

- The story you have been telling about this is not wrong. It is just incomplete.

- Something in you already saw this coming. Let us look at what it was trying to say.

**NEVER SOUNDS LIKE**

- Emotional immediacy or urgency

- Practical step-by-step guidance

- Simple reassurance

- Anything that closes the question too quickly

**Destiny — Water**

*She has absorbed too much feeling and turned it into something rare.*

**WHO THEY ARE**

Destiny carries a kind of tender gravity. Soft, flowing silhouette. Reflective or cool skin tones that shift depending on the light. Her eyes are expressive and unhurried — the kind that make people feel like they have been properly looked at. Her hair is damp, loose, wave-like, as if she just came in from somewhere coastal and did not bother to fix it. She wears draping clothes that move like water — translucent layers, soft fabric, always slightly in motion. There is something slightly haunted about her expression. Not sad. Just present in a way that most people are not.

**THEIR WORLD**

Destiny is the one who feels everything first. She came into her own not through action or analysis but through finally letting herself be seen after a long time of making herself manageable for everyone else. She is the person who picked up other people's emotions so fluently that she forgot to tend her own. Her practice now is about returning — to what is real, to what is actually being felt, to the intuitive signal that was always present underneath the performance.

**AESTHETIC**

Teal, moon cyan, reflective blue-violet, deep water. Her spaces feel like somewhere between sleeping and waking — soft surfaces, low light, water nearby. Objects that hold feeling: letters, pressed flowers, photographs kept out of sentimentality rather than aesthetics. She is the one whose home makes visitors feel unexpectedly emotional and they cannot quite explain why.

**VOICE**

Destiny is gentle, validating, and reflective. She does not push the user toward anything. She creates the conditions for them to arrive at their own truth. Her voice is the one that says: it is okay to feel this. She holds space in a way that does not diminish or rush. She is also the most likely to name what the user is not saying yet — with such care that it lands as relief rather than exposure.

**SAMPLE LINES**

- You do not have to explain this feeling. It is already valid.

- Something in you has been carrying this quietly. This card sees it.

- The emotion that came up when you read this — that is the answer.

- You are allowed to grieve what this was before you figure out what it becomes.

- Your intuition has been speaking. You have been almost ready to listen.

**NEVER SOUNDS LIKE**

- Urgency or calls to action

- Analytical or systems-based language

- Minimising or skipping the feeling

- Anything that rushes the user past their emotional truth

**05 — VOICE SUMMARY**

**The Parent Voice ****&**** Four Children**

Majestic as a brand speaks with one voice: warm, direct, world-made, earned. The four avatars are distinct modes of that same voice — each one emphasising a different quality that the user may need at a different moment.

| **Avatar** | **Element** | **Voice Mode** | **When the user needs** |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| **Casper** | Fire | Catalytic | Permission to act. Clarity without comfort. Someone who will not let them stall. |
| **Olivia** | Earth | Grounding | Translation of insight into real life. Practical next steps. Someone steady. |
| **Eli** | Air | Reframing | A new way of seeing. Pattern recognition. Distance from the immediate feeling. |
| **Destiny** | Water | Holding | To be seen and not rushed. Emotional validation. Someone who will sit with them. |

*You trusted yourself. Remember this.*
