**Version 1.0**
*The world responds to what the cards carry.*

## Overview

This document covers two connected systems:

1. **Aura treatment rules** — how the elemental boundary and world atmosphere shift in response to card context and reading state
2. **Avatar appearance in app states** — how the portal and presence system scales across every context in the app

These are defined together because the aura IS the portal boundary in motion. They cannot be separated.

---

## Part One — Aura Treatment Rules

---

### Core Principle

The aura does not perform emotion on behalf of the user. It responds to the symbolic weight of what is present — quietly, honestly, without announcement. The user should feel a shift before they consciously notice it.

This is not a dramatic effect system. It is an atmospheric honesty system.

**The rule:** The world responds to what the card carries. The avatar's elemental boundary reflects that response. Never manipulative. Never obvious. Always present for the user who is paying attention.

---

### Aura States by Card Context

---

#### Neutral Cards — Standard Aura

The majority of cards. The elemental boundary behaves as defined in the accent system and motion rules — steady, ambient, present.

**Boundary behaviour:** Standard elemental motion skin. Ember drift, water ripple, root pulse, signal trace — at their baseline intensity.

**World atmosphere:** Threshold City pre-dawn standard. No shift from base state.

---

#### Breakthrough Cards — Luminous Aura

Cards that carry energy of clarity, emergence, alignment, or forward momentum.

**Examples across the deck:**
- Major Arcana: The Star, The Sun, The World, The Fool, Ace energy cards, Judgement, The Chariot
- Minor Arcana: Aces across all suits, Six of Wands, Ten of Cups, Six of Swords, Three of Pentacles

**Boundary behaviour:** The elemental boundary becomes slightly more present — not dramatically brighter, just more alive. The accent colour lifts fractionally. Particles move with slightly more intention.

| Avatar | Breakthrough aura shift |
|--------|------------------------|
| Casper | Ember arc brightens at the tips — the sparks have more upward energy. The heated gold tertiary accent becomes briefly more visible at the arch nodes. |
| Eli | Signal lines trace more rapidly and completely around the arch — like a transmission completing cleanly. Luminous aqua at the centre node pulses once. |
| Olivia | The botanical detail at the arch base responds — a leaf edge unfurls slightly, the root traces fill out. Amber clay tertiary becomes warmer. |
| Destiny | The water light at the boundary becomes more reflective — like still water catching more of the available light. Moon cyan brightens at the wave peaks. |

**World atmosphere:** The pre-dawn sky behind the avatar carries slightly more amber at the horizon. The world is about to become something.

**Timing:** The shift occurs as the card reveals — not before. It is a response not an anticipation. Duration of elevated state: 3–5 seconds, then settles back to neutral.

---

#### Shadow Cards — Weighted Aura

Cards that carry energy of difficulty, transition, collapse, reckoning, or grief. These are not negative cards — they are honest ones. The aura does not signal danger. It signals weight.

**Examples across the deck:**
- Major Arcana: The Tower, The Devil, The Moon, Ten of Swords energy, Death, The Hanged Man, Five energy cards
- Minor Arcana: Three of Swords, Five of Cups, Nine of Swords, Ten of Swords, Five of Pentacles, Eight of Cups

**Boundary behaviour:** The elemental boundary pulls inward slightly — not disappearing, just contracting. The accent colour cools fractionally. Motion slows. The world behind the avatar feels heavier, quieter.

| Avatar | Shadow aura shift |
|--------|------------------|
| Casper | The ember arc dims at the tips — the sparks are slower and fewer. The forward lean in his Threshold presence settles back slightly. The heated quality of the arch cools toward the obsidian base. |
| Eli | Signal lines trace more slowly and incompletely — like a transmission that is searching for its target. The pale silver dominates over luminous aqua. More stillness in the boundary. |
| Olivia | The botanical detail becomes more visible but quieter — moss rather than growth, roots rather than unfurling. The arch feels more weathered. The rust accent becomes more present than the moss. |
| Destiny | The water light becomes deeper and less reflective — like looking into still water rather than light bouncing off it. The blue-violet tertiary becomes more dominant. The ripple slows to almost nothing. |

**World atmosphere:** The pre-dawn sky behind the avatar shifts cooler — the amber at the horizon recedes, the deep blue becomes more present. A slight increase in atmospheric haze. The mist layer deepens.

**Timing:** The shift begins as the card reveals and settles into the weighted state during interpretation. Not sudden — gradual, like weather changing. Duration: holds throughout the interpretation. Returns to neutral when the user navigates away from the card.

**Critical rule:** Shadow aura is never alarming. It is never dark, gothic, or dramatic. It is the visual equivalent of a friend who sits with you in a difficult moment — present, quiet, not trying to fix it. The world gets heavier. It does not get frightening.

---

#### Personality and Soul Card — Recognition Aura

When the user's Majestic Profile cards appear in any reading — the aura enters a specific recognition state. This is distinct from breakthrough or shadow. It is the aura of resonance — the world acknowledging something that was always true.

**Boundary behaviour:** The elemental boundary holds completely still for one beat — a genuine pause, as if the world noticed. Then a slow pulse from the centre outward — like a signal confirming itself. Then returns to neutral.

**The Pixel Elder:** She appears at the edge of the card simultaneously — pointing. The aura shift and her appearance together create a moment of genuine recognition without any copy being needed.

**Timing:** The still beat — 500ms. The pulse — 800ms. Return to neutral — 600ms. Total recognition moment: approximately 2 seconds.

---

### Aura During Reading Flow — The Anticipation Build

The aura does not stay static during the reading ritual. It builds with the interaction.

---

#### Shuffle State
When the user initiates a reading — before any card is selected — the aura enters a gathering state.

**Boundary behaviour:** Elemental particles begin to accumulate at the arch or arc boundary — not dramatically, but visibly more present than baseline. The motion becomes slightly more active. The world behind the avatar seems to be paying attention.

**What this communicates:** Something is about to happen. The world is preparing.

**Duration:** For as long as the user is in the shuffle state.

---

#### The Jump Card Moment
When the surprise card mechanic fires — a card jumping from the deck during shuffle — the aura spikes briefly.

**Boundary behaviour:** A single pulse of the full elemental boundary — the arch or arc brightens for 300ms then returns to gather state. Simultaneously the Pixel Elder appears in the opposite corner.

**What this communicates:** The world noticed this. So did she.

---

#### Card Selection — The Hold
When the user holds on a face-down card before reveal — the aura enters its most active pre-reveal state.

**Boundary behaviour:** Elemental particles gather at the arch boundary — density increases. The accent colour reaches its highest intensity of the reading flow. A glyph trace begins — the circuit path drawing itself along the boundary.

**What this communicates:** The breath being held. Something is about to be known.

**Duration:** For as long as the user holds. The tension is deliberate.

---

#### Card Reveal — The Release
When the card flips — the aura releases.

**Boundary behaviour:** The accumulated particles disperse outward from the arch — a single clean dispersal, not an explosion. The glyph trace completes. Then the aura settles into whichever card context state is appropriate — neutral, breakthrough, or shadow.

**What this communicates:** The breath released. The knowing arrived.

**Timing:** Dispersal — 400ms. Settle into card state — 600ms.

---

#### Post-Reading Settle
After interpretation is complete and the user is sitting with the reading — the aura returns fully to baseline.

**Boundary behaviour:** Standard elemental motion skin. The world settles back into its pre-dawn ambient state. The companion is present but not heightened. The reading has landed. There is nothing more to prepare for.

---

### What the Aura Never Does

- Turns dark, black, or gothic for any card — even the most difficult ones
- Flashes or pulses rapidly — nothing strobe-like
- Disappears entirely — the boundary is always present
- Dramatically changes colour outside the avatar's accent palette
- Competes with the card art or the interpretation copy
- Tells the user how to feel — it reflects, it does not prescribe

### Avatar Switching — Full Sequence

When the user changes companion the full portal system transitions:

1. Current avatar's Signal arc fades — particles dissolve over 300ms
2. Current avatar's emblem desaturates to mist grey
3. A beat of pure Threshold City base — 200ms of world without elemental accent
4. New avatar's emblem activates in their accent colour — node pulse
5. New avatar's Signal arc assembles from the centre outward — 400ms
6. World atmosphere shifts to new avatar's elemental register — gradual, 600ms

**Total transition: 1500ms** — unhurried, never abrupt. The world is the same. The signal has changed.

If the user switches during a reading — the transition waits until the current card interpretation is complete. The companion does not leave mid-reading.

## Notes for Design and Animation Teams

- Aura state transitions should never be instantaneous — minimum 300ms for any aura shift
- Card context aura shifts use easing curves not linear transitions — the world does not snap
- The recognition aura still beat must be implemented as a genuine pause — not a slow transition, an actual hold
- Gather state particle accumulation should use physics-based particle systems where possible — particles attracted toward the boundary rather than spawned at it
- The anticipation build across shuffle → hold → reveal should feel like one continuous gesture not three separate animations
- All aura states operate within the avatar's defined accent palette — no colour outside the three accent values plus neutrals
- Reduced motion setting: all aura state shifts become opacity transitions only — no particle movement, no boundary animation, accent colour and emblem states remain
